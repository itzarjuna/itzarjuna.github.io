Car Fuel Types & Cylinders impact on Mileage

The following is a narrative visualization of data that helps a car buyer make a informed decision on what car gives the best mileage. The visualization helps answer the question on the correlation between Fuel type & Mileage. Does a particular fuel type give a better mileage than other fule types. Also How does the Fule type compare to Mileage on Highway versus City driving. The dataset used for this excercise is cars.csv provided by the university.

The three links provide bar chart graphs for each fuel type.

Electric

Click on Electric to see the electric cars performmance on Highway and City. For this I've used a bar chart to show the car maker on x-axis and Average Mileage on Y-axis for Highway or City driving.

Gasoline

Click on Gasoline to see the Gasoline cars performmance on Highway and City For Gasoline Cars there is a scatterplot to show the correlation between # of cylinders and the cars mileage.

Diesel

Click on Diesel to see the Diesel cars performmance on Highway and City For Diesel Cars there is a scatterplot to show the correlation between # of cylinders and the cars mileage.
